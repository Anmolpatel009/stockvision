🎯 What is Market Regime?
Market regime describes the current market state based on trend and volatility patterns. Each regime requires different trading strategies.
⚡ Regime Types:
• TREND_STRONG: Strong directional movement - trend following works best
• TREND_WEAK: Weak trend - use momentum indicators
• MEAN_REVERTING: Prices return to average - contrarian strategies
• HIGH_VOLATILITY: Large price swings - reduce position sizes
• LOW_VOLATILITY: Calm markets - breakout strategies may fail
• UNKNOWN: Insufficient data - stay cautious
